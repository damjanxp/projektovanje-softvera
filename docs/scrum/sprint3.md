# Sprint 3 Plan — Cart & Purchase

## Sprint Goal
Implementirati kupovinu: pregled tura, korpa, bonus poeni i potvrda kupovine sa email-om.

## Sprint Backlog (US)
- US-C1 Korpa (add/remove) + total
- US-C2 Bonus poeni (apply to 0 min)
- US-C3 Purchase + email potvrda

## Deliverable (Inkrement)
- Turista može dodati ture u korpu i potvrditi kupovinu
- Bonus poeni se pravilno koriste i čuvaju
- Email potvrda se šalje (može koristiti “dev email provider”/log)

## Demo Scenario
1) Login tourist
2) View published tours
3) Add to cart, remove
4) Apply bonus points
5) Confirm purchase -> email/log output
